About Checkers

Established in 1956, Checkers is one of South Africa's leading supermarket chains under the Shoprite Group. We serve millions of customers across the country, offering a wide range of groceries, household items, and premium products.

Our mission is to provide quality products at competitive prices while delivering an exceptional customer experience. We strive to be the most trusted and innovative supermarket brand in South Africa.

Checkers targets middle- to upper-income households and young professionals who value quality, convenience, and affordability. Our commitment to innovation is demonstrated through services like Checkers Sixty60, which delivers groceries in 60 minutes or less.
