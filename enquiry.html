Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order for delivery?

You can place an order for delivery through our Checkers Sixty60 app, available on iOS and Android. Simply download the app, browse products, and schedule your delivery.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, seven days a week. You can choose a delivery slot that works best for you during the checkout process.

How can I join the Xtra Savings program?

You can join the Xtra Savings program by visiting any Checkers store and filling out an application form, or you can register online through our website.
